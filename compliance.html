The rest of the kitchen, kept compliant.

The recurring compliance and asset-maintenance services that keep a commercial kitchen audit-ready — scoped to your site and delivered on one plan, one invoice.

The whole obligation

Compliance doesn't stop at the canopy.

A commercial kitchen carries obligations across fire-safety measures, trade-waste, grease traps and asset condition — each one with a record someone has to keep, and each one a place a lapse can surface at audit.

Summit consolidates them onto one planned program with one accountable partner. The services stay on cycle, the records stay current, and the whole obligation is carried in a single place rather than tracked across a dozen.

01  What's included

One program across
every obligation.

Site audits & inspections

Documented compliance and condition visits on cycle — each obligation checked, recorded and evidenced against its standard.

Grease trap & trade-waste

Scheduled servicing kept to cycle, with the records your trade-waste agreement asks you to hold and produce on request.

Fire-safety measures · AS 1851

Extinguishers, fire blankets, and emergency & exit lighting maintained and tested to AS 1851, with certification held on file.

Annual compliance statements

Evidence aggregated across the year and held ready to lodge — including the maintenance records a NSW Annual Fire Safety Statement assessment relies on.

Each service is scoped per site, and some are delivered with accredited partners — coordinated by Summit under one plan.

02  Asset protection

Problems fixed at the asset,
not noted at the audit.

Two upgrades close the most common gaps an exhaust record exposes — supplied, installed and kept on cycle by the same crew that services the system.

Rooftop grease containment

Containment fitted around the exhaust fan catches discharge before it reaches the roof — protecting the membrane, the drainage and the building owner's insurance position.

Pads replaced on cycle

Containment media saturates and quietly stops working. Ours is checked and replaced on the same cycle as the exhaust service — photographed to the record like everything else.

Access panels, supplied & installed

Where a duct run can't be reached, we fit compliant access — so the next service cleans the full length instead of declaring the same limitation twice.

Limitations, closed

Every limitation declared on a Summit record arrives with the remedy quoted. Fix it once, and the next certificate carries no caveat.
