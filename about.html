The maintenance partner, not the callout.

Summit is an independent NSW company built around one idea — a commercial kitchen's compliance shouldn't live in someone's memory. It should be planned, documented and owned.

Who we are

We maintain the assets a kitchen depends on — and the evidence that proves it.

Most kitchens buy cleaning. Summit provides assurance — the difference between a job that was done and a record that proves it, held current for the audit, the insurer and the renewal.

We're built for the whole obligation, not one part of it: exhaust compliance to AS 1851-2012, filter restoration, and the recurring services that keep a site audit-ready — coordinated under one plan, with one accountable partner.
