High-access cleaning, without the scaffold.

Façade and pressure cleaning delivered by drone — no scaffold, no cherry-picker, with condition imagery captured as it works. In readiness across NSW.

In readiness

A safer way to reach what others can't.

High-access façade and pressure cleaning normally means scaffold, EWP hire and the risk that comes with working at height. Drone delivery removes the access problem — and captures condition imagery for the building record while it works.

This is a capability Summit is standing up: a CASA-credentialed operation, launching to existing compliance clients first. When it is live, high-access work becomes a planned service rather than a project.

01  What's included

Reach, cleaned
and recorded.

Façade & high-access cleaning

Glass, cladding and rendered elevations cleaned at height — the surfaces access equipment struggles to reach, worked without it.

Pressure cleaning, hard-to-reach

Pressure cleaning of awnings, soffits and upper surfaces that would otherwise need scaffold or a cherry-picker to service.

Condition imagery captured

High-resolution imagery of the elevation gathered as the drone works — fed straight into the asset record for the building.

No scaffold, no EWP

No scaffold, no elevated work platform, less disruption on site — and the working-at-height exposure taken off the ground.
