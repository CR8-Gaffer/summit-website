Privacy statement.

Last updated · July 2026

Who we are

Summit Asset Maintenance Pty Ltd (“Summit”, “we”) provides commercial kitchen compliance and asset-maintenance services across Sydney and Greater NSW. We handle personal information in line with the Australian Privacy Principles. [ABN — to be confirmed]

What we collect, and why

The only personal information this website collects is what you choose to send us through the enquiry form or by contacting us directly:

  • Your name, business or venue name, email address and phone number.
  • Anything you include in your message about your site and its requirements.

We use it for one purpose: responding to your enquiry and, if you engage us, arranging and delivering the services you've asked about. We don't sell it, rent it, or use it for unrelated marketing.

Cookies & analytics

This site does not set cookies and does not run analytics or advertising trackers.

How enquiries are handled

Form submissions are delivered to our enquiries inbox (where a form-processing provider is used to deliver them, it processes your submission for that purpose only). Enquiry details are kept only as long as needed to respond and to service any engagement that follows.

Service records

If you become a client, the service records we produce for your sites contain operational information about your premises and nominated contacts. Those records are held for you — they exist to be produced to your insurer, assessor or auditor at your request — and are retained in line with compliance record-keeping expectations.

Access, correction & questions

You can ask us at any time what personal information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete enquiry details we no longer need. Contact us at enquiries@summitam.com.au or on 1300 000 000.
